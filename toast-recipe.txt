1 piece of bread
1 toaster
1 selection of condiment, i.e., butter, jelly, jam, etc.
1 utensil

directions:
	1) put bread in toaster
	2) set toaster heat level (you figure it out)
	3) push toaster button to begin toasting
	4) wait - but don't watch the toaster. Remember - the watched pot never boils.
	5) apply the condiment of your choice
	6) enjoy
